Taking notes has become a daily occurance. From growing to-do lists to meeting notes, I often finding myself waiting for the software to keep up. Most note-taking apps are somewhat cumbersome to use so I set out to build a notepad built for speed. Introducing Jot:

With Jot, you write and note and you move on. No unnecessary taps, no fancy menus. Just an easy way to jot down a thought and get back to your life.

To write a new note using Jot, you simply swipe right-to-left. Want to see all of your old notes? Swipe the other way.
