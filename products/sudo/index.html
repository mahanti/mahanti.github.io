Sudo Beautiful Sudoku — with a colorful twist.
About Sudo is a simple Sudoku app that let's you play Sudoku with colors in addition to numbers. This classic spin, paired with delightful interactions and animations, makes it a must have for any Sudoku lover.

The two modes work seamlessly between each other.

Sudo keeps track of basic statistics for all of your puzzles.

It wouldn’t be an indie app without a few custom icons.
