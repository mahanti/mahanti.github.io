Senior Product Designer, Square for Restaurants

Square Kitchen Display

Square’s Kitchen Display System (KDS) is built to help expeditors, kitchen managers, and cooks run a more efficient service. It combines the speed and flexibility of paper tickets with the Square platform. From managing courses for Table 5 to meeting the rush of online orders on a rainy day, KDS keeps kitchen weeds at bay.

I lead the product design work for KDS alongside Mikael Westman and Matt Kursmark. We worked closely with chefs and restaurantuers to design a product that not only gives chefs more control in the kitchen but also streamlines overall operational tasks. Through numerous prototypes and iterations we landed on a system that masked complexity while allowing flexibility and speed.
