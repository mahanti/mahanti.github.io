Sidecar

Sidecar was the first ridesharing service founded in 2011. I joined shortly after the company's inception as the first full-time designer. In my years there I worked on everything from ground-up redesigns of our Driver and Rider apps to marketing and out-of-home advertising campaigns. Sidecar was eventually acquired by General Motors in 2016.
