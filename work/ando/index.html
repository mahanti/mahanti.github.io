Ando

Ando was a delivery-first restaurant created by David Chang in partnership with Expa and the Momofuku restaurant group. I led the design efforts across both marketing and product design. For diners, we focused on creating new ways of ordering and interacting with a ghost kitchen. For chefs, we built integrated kitchen displays and menu management systems to help them throughout service. Ando was acquired by Uber in 2017.
