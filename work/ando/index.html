Ando Ando was a delivery-only restaurant by Momofuku, in partnership with Expa. During my time there, I was responsible for designing multiple zero-to-one products, from the app customers used to place orders to the menu management system chefs used to track inventory. I also led our food photography and packaging efforts.

Ando was acquired by Uber in 2017.

The Ando customer app interface for ordering food delivery.

I worked with Christina Tosi and the Milkbar team to design packaging for our co-branded Milkbar cookies.

I was the food photographer for Ando, taking photos for both menu items and marketing materials.
