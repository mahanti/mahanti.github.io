This is an email header created for a Sidecar promotion we ran during ComicCon in San Diego, CA. While I was pleased with the character of the final piece it ultimately did not perform well in the field. This was due to my lack of understanding of Facebook's guidelines for marketing images. Apparently if an image contains more than X% text it's flagged and not distributed as broadly. It's always a bummer when that something like that happens but you live and your learn.
